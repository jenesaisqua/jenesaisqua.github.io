About

高雄人，雙子座，O型。目前就讀世新大學資訊傳播學系，興趣是追劇、追星，平常就是個大迷妹，非常崇洋媚外，東西只要掛上made in 韓國或日本就覺得是品質保證，一定會立馬丟入購物車。然後我是一個喜歡嘗試新事物的人，喜歡參加各式各樣的活動。自認個性活潑開朗、喜歡交朋友，但都沒有很多人要跟我交朋友，上大學立志擺脫邊緣人。喜歡打排球，但不喜歡運動。平常最擅長待在宿舍當一坨會呼吸的肉，然後耍花癡。

I come from Kaohsiung. My constellation is Gemini. Blood type is O. I study in Shih Hsin University of Information and Communications. My interest are watching korean drama and listening K-pop.I really like Korea and Japan. Then I am a person who likes to try new things and likes to participate in a variety of activities. I think that I am a lively and cheerful person, likeing to make friends with everyone. Like playing volleyball, but do not like to do excercise.

University

世野媒體工作室

開學時參加了世新美食地圖，認識了一位傳管大三的學姊，聽他說他想創一個社團，主要是和媒體有關，我就參加了，因為我們是在期中才完成創社的程序，所以我們錯過了期初的社團招生，每次社課都只有個位數的人，不過都是大家都是從一開始就對這個社團有興趣，所以人數雖少，但大家都很認真地在上課。我們上學期的社課中教了採訪、直播、攝影、剪輯相關課程。

I participated in an activity when I started school, meeting a schoolmate. She said that she wanted to create a club about mass media. Because we completed the program of creating the club during the middle term, we missed the beginning of the year for the recruitment of societies. There were only single-digit members each time, but everyone was interested in this community from the very beginning. Although the number is small, everyone is taking lessons seriously. We taught courses in interviews, live broadcasts, photography, and editing during our last semester's social class.

漫畫研究社

從小我就很喜歡動漫，一直很想加入漫研社看看裡面到底在幹嘛，是不是真的就是每堂社課都在看動畫，而且還可以認識很多志同道合的朋友，但是我們國中社團沒有漫研社，所以一上高中我最期待的就是加入漫研成為當中的一份子。雖然上了之後並不像我想像中那樣，社課大部分都在教繪畫，對於美術能力零的我來說根本就是另一個世界，但我還是成為了幹部，三年的社課學到了很多繪畫技巧和化妝技巧，最重要的是交到了很多朋友。

I have always liked animation since I was young. I have always wanted to join the comics research club to see what they are doing. If every club class is watching animations, and I can also meet many like-minded friends, but our junior high school don’t have the club. So what I am most looking forward to as soon as I go to senior high school is to join the club and become a part of it. Although it was not as I imagined, most of the courses taught painting. For me who didn't paint at all, it was another world. But I still became a leader. I learned a lot from the three years of club affairs. Painting skills and make-up skills, the most important thing is to make a lot of friends.

氣球魔術社

當初會想加入的原因是因為會教怎麼折氣球，覺得折氣球感覺超厲害的，每次看到路邊的街頭藝人在折氣球就很想學怎麼折，可是也不知道去哪裡學，然後剛好看到學校社團有開所以我就加入了，我們的社師真的是請外面的街頭藝人來，就是個年輕長得很可愛的男生，在這兩年的社課中真的學到了很多氣球的折法，那時候只要遇到認識的人我就會折氣球給他，他就會覺得我超厲害哈哈哈，雖然我們還有教魔術，但是都是教一些簡單的道具魔術，所以我對國中社團印象最深的就是學會折了很多氣球。

The reason why I would like to join in the beginning is because it will teach me how to fold a balloon. I feel that folding balloon is awesome. Every time I see a street performers, I would like to learn how to fold it, but I don’t know where to go, and then I just saw that the school club had an opening so I joined. Our club teacher really wanted to come outside the street performers. It was a young, lovely-looking boy who made me really learn a lot during the two years of club classes. At that time, as soon as I meet someone who knows me, I will give him a folded balloon. He will think that I am awesome. Although we still teach magic, we teach some simple prop magic, so the deepest impression on the junior high school club was that I learned to fold a lot of balloons.

Interests

flight

我喜歡到處去旅行，不管是國內外。我覺得旅行能讓自己增廣見聞，認識不同地方的文化，而且旅行也是種休息，雖然不一定是身體休息，但一定是心理的歇息。我很喜歡自助旅行，因為可以自己安排想去的地方，想待多久就待多久。

I like to travel everywhere, whether at home or abroad. I think that travel can make me more knowledgeable, understand the culture of different places, and travel is a kind of rest. Although it is not necessarily a physical rest, it must be a psychological rest. I really like to travel by myself because I can arrange where I want to go and how long I would like to stay.

camera_alt

旅行時一定會拍各種照片，證明自己到過那裡，所以比起拍人像，我更喜歡拍風景。我喜歡藉由相機拍下各種瞬間，不論是美好的還是不美好的，按下快門後都可以保存當下。之前沒有很認真地在拍照，都是看到覺得可以就拍，但是自從上了攝影課後知道構圖的重要，讓我的攝影從生活的紀錄走向藝術的創作。

When I travel, I will take various photos to prove that I have been there, so I like to take landscape more than people. I like to capture various moments with the camera. Whether it is good or bad, you can save the moment when you press the shutter button. I didn't take photos very seriously before. I saw that I could shoot, but I knew the importance of composition since I was in the photography class, and I moved my photography from the record of life to the creation of art.

audiotrack

我喜歡聽音樂，尤其是K-POP。我覺得聽歌是一件很舒壓的事，不管在心情好不好的時候都可以聽歌，而且每首歌都有它的意義，所以當我在聽那首歌的時候，我會去思考創作者為時麼要寫這首歌，不是為了要紅，而是要去紀念某件事，這也是我追韓國團體的原因，因為他們想藉由音樂去影響大家，讓大家知道現在這個社會怎麼了，所以音樂對我不只是單純的舒壓，而是有更深的意義。

I like to listen to music, especially K-POP. I think listening to music is a very comfortable matter. You can listen to music whether your mood is good or not, and each song has its meaning, so when I listen to that song, I will think about it. And when I listen the song I would think why the creator wants to write this song is not for popular, but for commemorating something. This is why I’m tempted by Korean groups because they want to influence people through music and let everyone know how this society is now. Well, music is not only a relief to me, but it has a deeper meaning.

Dreams

其實我沒什麼夢想，但希望我以後可以賺很多錢，然後到處去旅行、買自己想要的東西、定居在韓國瘋狂的追星，所以如果硬要說的話，我的夢想就是賺大錢，因為我覺得沒有錢夢想再偉大都是枉然，所以我認真讀書只為了畢業之後找到一份好工作，然後過自己想要的生活。

Actually, I don't have any dreams, but I hope I can make a lot of money later, and then I travel around, buy what I want, and settle in Korea. So if I have to say it, my dream is to make a lot of money, because I think no money, dreams how great are in vain, so I study hard only to find a good job after graduation, and then live the life I want.
